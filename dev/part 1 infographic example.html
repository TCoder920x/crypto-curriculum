Wallet Types: Control vs. Convenience

Choosing a wallet is your first major decision. It boils down to a trade-off: do you want full control over your funds, or the convenience of a third-party service?

Custodial

The Future: Governance & Advanced Concepts

Lifecycle of a DAO Proposal

Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs) are governed by their members. This timeline shows the transparent, community-driven process of making decisions, from initial idea to on-chain execution.

1. Proposal Creation

A community member drafts a formal proposal outlining a change or new initiative and submits it for consideration.

2. Community Discussion

The proposal is debated on forums and community calls. Feedback is gathered, and amendments may be made.

3. On-Chain Voting

Members use their governance tokens to vote for or against the proposal. The weight of their vote is often proportional to their token holdings.

4. Execution

If the vote passes the required threshold, the proposal's smart contract is automatically executed, implementing the change on the blockchain.
